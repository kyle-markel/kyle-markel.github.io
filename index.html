About

A little bit about myself.

  • 1997-2011

    Childhood

    Growing up I had always been interested in art, and dabbled with various forms over the years, mostly drawing.

  • 2012-2016

    High School

    By high school, I had begun to take art as a career more seriously, and learned to use tools like Photoshop and Blender.

  • September 2016

    College Beginnings

    When I started college, I wasn't sure which form of art I wanted to specialize in, but the classes I took at Messiah helped shape me into who I am today.

  • December 2019

    Present Day

    Presently, I have learned that I don't want to focus on just one thing, so I've decided to keep my options open and work with whatever I enjoy most.
